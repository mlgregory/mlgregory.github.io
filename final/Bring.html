BRING

Have you ever been locked out of your apartment? Forget your midterm the day its due? Well here's your solution! Introducing Bring! A service that reminds you of your essential items before you leave. With a beacon placed above your door, Bring will notify you of what items you wanted fo rthe day. If you don't have your phone the beacon will sound with a noise. If you do have your phone it'll send you your essentials list to remind you to grab what you need for the day. A project for Design Research at MassArt.
