A book combining three pieces of writing all focused on the theme of drowning/suffocation. To show both sides of the coin, I paired Houdini's last trick letter on his coffin in a pool tirck, which touches upon lose of air and fear of drowning. Along side this I chose a letter by a trapped miner, his letter is his final words to his family. The third piece to tie it all togeter is the stages of drowning. I brought these particular stories together to show multiple ideas of loose of air and concisousess. The book has some pages white with black type and others have black pages with black type, showing the fading of conciousness as the perosn loses air while writing their letters. A project for Typography III at MassArt.
