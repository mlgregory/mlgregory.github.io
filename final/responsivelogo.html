Choose a client within a sector of your choice and design a visual identity system that includes a dynamic mark that responds to a set of data. For this project I chose to work with the New England Revolution soccer team. This team is one of 22 in Major League Soccer in the US. Founded in 1996, the team created a freef lowing flag logo with a soccer ball to hold the stars. It has been sloghtly modified over the years. The team is the only one without a crest for a logo.
