NARRATIVE AS DATA

The goal of this project was to develop an educational environment for those who study cinema and "new media" in order to help the user to understand and explore the content and structure of Rear Window by Alfred Hitchcock. After watching the movie multiple times and collecting data from the movie, we designed a interactive browser to explore the musical clips from Rear Window, and compare various characters and their relation to music. In addition to the interface, we made a poster to explain how it works and all the elements that can be explored. A project for Typography III at MassArt.
