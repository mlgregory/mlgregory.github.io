Meyer Lemon Relish

with Cranberry and Pomegranate

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups cranberries (about 14 ounces)
  • 1 cup pure pomegranate juice
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup fresh Meyer lemon juice or regular
  • 2 tbsp finely grated Meyer lemon peel or regular
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Combine first five ingredients in large saucepan. Stir over medium-high heat until sugar dissolves.
  2. Bring to boil.
  3. Reduce heat to medium; simmer until berries begin to burst, stirring often, about 10 minutes.
  4. Transfer to small bowl.
  5. Stir in parsley.
  6. Cover and chill until cold
  • Can be made 3 days ahead. Keep chilled
  • Makes 3 cups
